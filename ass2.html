User Guide

The objective of the game is to acquire more money than the AI super genius you are competing against! You do this by acquiring land and using robot workers called 'roboticons' to produce resources for you to be sold at the market for a tidy profit.

The game is broken down into two main phases. In the first phase both you and he AI acquire a plot, get roboticons and have them generate resources. It is randomly decided who gets to go first. While in the second phase you both access he market simultaneously to either purchase resources from the market, or to sell what your roboticons have produced for you.

Step 1. The player selects an unoccupied plot of land from the map.

Step 2. The player is then prompted with the market, and within the turn limit of 30 seconds the player may purchase roboticons or attempt to make the market produce more roboticons.

Step 3. The player is then prompted with roboticon customisation screen. This allows players to turn their uncustomised roboticons into a customised roboticon for a fee.

Step 4. With the timer from the previous step still going, the player is then allowed to place a customised roboticon on a tile they own by clicking on it and selecting which type of customised roboticon they wish to place. If the roboticon type placed is matching with the selected tiles specialisation then it produces two of that resources. Otherwise it will produce one resource of the roboticons type.

Step 5. The player can then idicate it is the end of their turn by clicking the 'Finished my turn' button located in the upper right of the screen.

Step 6. The player can now sell their resources just produced by their roboticons or they may buy some needed resources from the market if they wish.

Step 7. The round is now over and the player now repeats the previous steps.

Step 8. Once there are no unoccupied plots left it is game over. The players scores are totaled and a window presents the final scores along with who the winner is.
